## Condition Notes

Important condition details to check:

- Are both cassettes included?
- Is the booklet included?
- Is the outer box original?
- Is the outer box damaged, crushed, torn, or stained?
- Do the tapes play normally?
- Are the labels clean?
- Is there mold or water damage?
- Are the cassette shells cracked?
- Does the packaging still display well?

A complete set with damaged packaging may still be meaningful.

But it should not be judged the same way as a sealed or near-mint example.

## What Usually Weakens Value

This object may have weaker future value if it is:

- missing one cassette
- missing booklet
- missing original box
- heavily damaged
- moldy
- water damaged
- not playable
- fake or reproduced
- priced too high
- missing clear photos
- sold only on hype around the artist
- incomplete compared with other available examples
