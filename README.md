# Digital Age Antiques

A Human Judgment Guide to Future Collectibles, Early Digital Products, Pre-AI Objects, and AI-Era Physical Assets.

Created by Xufen Tu.

## What is Digital Age Antiques?

Digital Age Antiques studies which real physical objects may become valuable in the AI era.

These may include future collectibles, early digital products, pre-AI objects, vintage electronics, music collectibles, physical media, old toys, cameras, game consoles, boxed software, and everyday objects that carry authenticity, scarcity, provenance, cultural memory, and human experience.

## Core Idea

Not every old object becomes valuable.

An object may become valuable in the future when it carries:

- real-world authenticity
- cultural memory
- scarcity
- condition
- completeness
- original packaging
- provenance
- display value
- story value
- buyer demand
- human judgment

## Why this matters in the AI era

In an AI-generated world, images, text, voices, styles, and digital products can be copied quickly.

But real physical objects still carry material history, age, wear, ownership, packaging, production limits, and human memory.

Digital Age Antiques focuses on real objects that AI cannot fully copy or replace.
